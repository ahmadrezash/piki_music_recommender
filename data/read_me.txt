This archive contains the materials for the final assignment for
LT2001 H21

Gerlof Bouma, University of Gothenburg

The files are supporting materials for the development of a music
recommendation system. More information about the source of the data
can be found here:

  https://github.com/sstoikov/piki-music-dataset
  http://ceur-ws.org/Vol-2959/paper9.pdf


FILES

                          format, consisting of all 50 users from the PIKI

                          the reference data in toypiki_dataset.csv
